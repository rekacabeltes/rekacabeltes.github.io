About Me

My friends call me by my nickname "Kang" for short. I am 19 years old. I am born and raised in Cagayan de Oro City, The City of Golden Frienship. In our family, I am the third child. I have three brothers and I am the only girl in the family.

My habits are listening to music, play online games and i also loved to draw although im not good at it. I also enjoy watching movies and kdramas. I consider myself to be a person who views life positively in all respects. I enjoy a wide variety of activities, sights, and experiences. I am very adventurous and creative.

Im currently studying in The University of Science and Technology of Southern Philippines (USTP). My first day of college is the most memorable day for me, it marked the beginning of my desire to attend college and pursue a higher education. I had so many struggles and emotions rushing through me because it was my first time being in this level, I am not well prepared to be a college student. I know that college is difficult. It has a lot of responsibilities, lack of time management, and changes of eating and sleeping habits.

Eventhough college life would be hard for me, I still willing to take a risk and work hard for it. because in life, we should all be willing to overcome difficulties. Without taking a chance, you'll never find out. Risk-taking isn't bad at all, pushing your limits is beneficial, and you can discover new things if you strive to explore more.
